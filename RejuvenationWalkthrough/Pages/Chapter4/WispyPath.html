Along the initial stretch of Wispy Path you'll have to battle both Hardcore Trainer Michelle and Ghost Girl Anna. The path then splits north and south. We'll go ahead and go south first, and you'll be able to go up the stairs into a small park. The girl inside the house to the left will ask to see an Axew, which we don't have at the moment. You'll also see an Espurr, and behind it a Zygarde Cell. You can talk with the Espurr and play with it, and if you play with it enough it will join you when you try to leave the area. Giving it Ice Cream is a very quick way to gain friendship with it.

With that done, head back to the intersection and go north instead. You'll come across an entrance gate and Old Lady Lanya beside it. This gate will lead to Wispy Tower, and there's not much else to do on Wispy Path. There's an exit gate on the left that leads to Goldenwood Forest, but we'll explore that section later. For now, head to Wispy Tower, using the Healing Machine inside the gate if you need to.

There's not much to do in the Wispy Tower area. Head inside and talk to Narcissa, and examine the graves if you want to. After that, head back to Goldenleaf Town and talk with Narcissa and Aelita. As the three of you head to the gym, Narcissa and Aelita will be attacked by a Xen Executive, and you'll be blamed for it. Mosely will help you bust out of prison, and now we'll need to head back through Wispy Path and to Wispy Tower.

Wispy Path - Team Xen Attack



There's a Xen truck in Wispy Path now, so we'll have to battle two Xen Grunts, Team Xen George and Team Xen Loriel, to get to Wispy Tower. Go ahead and beat them. Team Xen George does have a Shadow Pokemon, but as our machine is broken at the moment we won't be able to catch it. Heal up in the gate, then proceed into Wispy Tower.
