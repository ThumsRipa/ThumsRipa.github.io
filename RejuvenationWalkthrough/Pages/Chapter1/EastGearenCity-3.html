On the left side, there's Team Xen Laurel in the train station area, Team Xen Orran in Leaflet Park, and Team Xen Yanny right outside Emerald Building. On the right side, there's Team Xen Vera below Luck's Tent, and Team Xen Teepo outside Aqua Building.

Other Activities


Before we move to the sidequests, there's a two other things we can do. If you head over to Violet Building, on the second floor this lady will allow you to take her Lillipup. Once you talk to her, interact with the Lillipup to obtain it.

Outside Violet Building, you'll also notice that the stairs has been unlocked. If you head down, you'll find yourself in a different part of the sewers. There's only one trainer down here, Technician Arnold. Before you exit through the north, there's a Rare Candy in the top left. Outside the north exit, on the left in the dumpsters is a Reverse Candy. If you came here during the day, the entrace will be blocked off. Coming here at night will give you access to an area where you can battle and capture Munna.

That's all the new areas we have access to. In the next part, we will be doing the sidequests available in East Gearen City. If you want to jump straight to Venam, you can do so here.
