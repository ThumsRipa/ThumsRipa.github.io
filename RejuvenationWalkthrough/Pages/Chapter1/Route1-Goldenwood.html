Route 1 & Goldenwood Forest

The Train Station


As you enter the train station, you'll meet Tesla again. She'll catch up with you for a bit, and then buys you a train ticket to Route 1. She'll also hand you a few Potions. Before we get on the train, you can talk to the old man on the left side or use the Pokemon Box near him. If you do have to return, future tickets will cost 100. Once you're ready, get on the train.

Route 1


You'll appear right on the Route 1 station. Head down stairs, but before turning right up to Melia, there's a Potion hidden in the shade of a tree. Be sure to also collect the Super Repel on the right. Head up and Melia will welcome you to Route 1, and will go ahead into Goldenwood Forest. There's a Whismur here that you can give a Gourmet Treat to for the opportunity to catch it. The encounters here will vary by time, but if you want a Rockruff then we'll be able to find those easier once we're in the forest proper. Battles here will take place in the Forest Field. You can also use this opportunity to heal with the golden star or use the Pokemon Box with the green star. Once you're reading, head up and into Goldenwood Forest.

Goldenwood Forest is pretty expansive, but also pretty linear. Go ahead and talk with Melia first, and she'll give you a short guided tour of the forest. The two of you will find yourself at the stairs leading up to Goldenwood Cave. Before entering though, grab the Potion, and you can also go back and fight Fisherman Shane and Fisherman Orlando. The grass here doesn't have any new encounters, but the rarity of the Pokemon would have changed from Route 1.


Before heading up into Goldenwood Cave, talk to the Driftblim a little to the left. You'll be taken into a short cutscene, and you'll get a HP Up for your troubles. Go back and switch up your Pokemon if you want, then head into Goldenwood Cave.

Similar to Gearen Sewers, you'll be paired up with Melia for most of this cave, and all the battles will be in the Doubles format. Additionally, battles here will be fought in the Cave field. The first set of trainers are Schoolboy Darin and Gang Member Emile. There's an item nearby, but we'll have to come back for it after we have Rock Smash. A little past them is Cool Guy Jeff and Youngster Nickolus, and then Optimist Kairi and Tourist Karin.. Near the end, before you go inbetween the narrow path by the two rocks, head up the stairs to the right. Here, you can find a two Tiny Mushrooms to sell later.

That's all there is to do, but you can use Melia to heal up your Pokemon while you are grinding levels. Once you walk past the two rocks, Melia will leave your party. After a short cutscene, she'll head off deeper into the cave. Before you follow her, you can also undergo Culvier Clan Mars' challenge. We won't be able to return here for a while after this, so if you want a Sewaddle now is the time.


Challenger Mars Without the right Pokemon, this battle can be very rough. Since the battle is fought in a Cave, Special Flying moves are reduced in power. Rock-type moves are boosted on this field, but Rock-type Pokemon will likely be taken down by the very powerful Seed Bomb that all of Mars' Pokemon carry. If Sewaddle gets a Light Screen off, taking down Mars' team can be very difficult. If you got a Fire starter, this battle will most likely be much easier. If you don't, consider going back to East Gearen City and grabbing a Nidoran of either gender as they can be very useful, Bug/Flying types can also do good damage, but will likely be taken down by Leavanny's damage output. If you manage to take down Leavanny though, the rest of her Pokemon do not have a large enough Attack stat to one hit KO your Pokemon.

Goldenwood Grove


There's not much to do here, the encounters are the same as in Goldenwood Forest. Go ahead and talk to Melia and enjoy the cutscene. Once that ends, Melia will ask you for a battle, so when you're ready go ahead and challenge her. This battle will be fought once again in the Forest Field.


Pre-Leader Melia If you've been doing a bit of grinding, your Pokemon should be higher level than her at this point. If your levels are even, then this battle could be a little challenging. Buneary carries Fire Punch, which is very strong at this point in the game, Eevee and Stufful can steal your berries, and Togepi has the possibility of getting stat boosts from Ancient Power. If you're struggling, you can level up and just out-stat her Normal-type team.
