Team Xen Battleship


The Battleship isn't a particularly large area. In the current room, you can go ahead and grab the Pokeball off the table, reading the letter and getting yourself a Mawile. Head out of the room, and the room to the right contains a Pokemon Breeder where you can train your Pokemon and also a shop. Do note that battles here take place on the Factory Field. Head out onto the deck and over to the right, where Melia will explain the gist of what's going on. She'll also ask to battle you, and head back inside to where the breeder is. You can walk around a bit, and on the second floor of the ship in the Captain's room you can find a Dull Key, which will let us catch a starter later. There's not much left to do, so head back inside and into the right room. Stand across from Melia to start the battle.

Overall not too difficult of a battle. Her Pokemon are generally on the bulky side, but can still dish out damage if you're not careful. You can take advantage of the field and use boosted Electric-type or certain Steel-type moves to deal massive damage to most of her team since she doesn't really resist either of those types. As long as you don't let Zoroark set up with Nasty Plot or let Togekiss ParaFlinch you to death, you should be able to comfortably win this battle.

Melia will leave onto the deck once you finish battling her. Take this opportunity to heal up and grind your extra Pokemon up, and when you're ready head out onto the deck and talk to Melia.
