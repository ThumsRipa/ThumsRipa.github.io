Upon entering, you'll find yourself in the middle of a volcano. Aelita will accompany you, and battles here are fought in the Volcanic Field and wild battles will be in a Doubles format while Aelita is with you. Like all partners, Aelita will heal you after every battle, so use this opportunity to train with the Exp Share if you need to. Go ahead and go straight through the area, on the top left there's Rock Smash rock that you can get a Carbos if you get past it. Head across the second bridge, and interact with the weird machine there. It'll introduce itself as SEC, a Team Xen invention. It'll also challenge you to a battle once it realizes you are not part of Team Xen.

Feel free to catch the Shadow Ponyta if you want, we'll be able to purify them very soon. After the battle SEC will self-terminate its current body and you'll be free to continue deeper inside.

Inside, you'll immediately face Team Xen Danie in a Double Battle with Aelita as your partner. Once you beat her, there's a PC nearby if you need to change out your Pokemon. Head left to battle Team Xen Zeneth, then up to battle Team Xen Timothy. Right above Timothy, there's a plaque that'll give you one of the passwords you'll need soon. You'll come across SEC again, so talk to SEC and battle him again.

He'll self-destruct again once you beat him, so move on forward. The left path will take you to a second password plaque, and the right side will take you to another intersection. Going right across the bridge will net you a Fire Stone. Heading down from the intersection will be TM43 - Flame Charge. This is a really good move at this point in the game, so teach it to anyone who can learn it. Beat Team Xen Ben to access the final password plaque. Go back to where you defeated SEC the second time, battle Team Xen Alyssa, and you'll have access to SEC again. He'll ask for the three passwords, which are "BEAUTY", "IDOL", "SHADOW", in that order with that capitalization. Once you beat him, he'll challenge you to yet another battle.

You and Aelita will appear outside a building, and once you get close enough Aelita will be kidnapped by SEC 2.0. Once you get inside, Keta will join you after Madelis explains to him what is going on. You'll have to find four keys, each in a corner of the laboratory. There aren't any wild Pokemon here, but trainer battles will be in a Doubles format, with no field in effect.

On the bottom left side, you'll first battle Team Xen Alison, then after grabbing the Hyper Potion head up to battle the duo of Xen Analyst Forrest and Xen Analyst Jensly. You can grab the Revive on the left side and the Yellow Key near the top, and there's an option battle against Team Xen Demil and Team Xen Aisel for extra EXP and gold if you want.

Head back out and into the bottom right room. Here, you can talk to Sharon, near the entrance, who will just give you the Green Key as she tries to rescue her partner. There's a Fresh Water in the top right corner of this room, and if you battle Team Xen Melly and Team Xen Sarah you can get access to a Mime Jr.

Our next area is the top left room. There's only one battle here against Xen Analyst Donald and Xen Analyst Maria. Beat them and grab the Red Key.

The final area is in the top right room. At the intersection, the path upwards against Team Xen Jessie and Team Xen Jasmine leads to the Blue Key. The path on the left against Xen Analyst Fern and Xen Analyst Prescilla. lets you grab an Upgrade.

Now that you have all the keys, insert them into the keyholes around SEC 2.0. You can also talk to the SEC 2.0 for some fun lore bits. Head on deeper, and meet Madelis and Aelita inside. Watch the scene unfold, then follow Keta deeper into the building. Heal up with the healing machine if you need to. Be sure to save, then enter the back rooms. Madelis will be waiting, and after she realses Aelita, will show off her Shadow Mewtwo, destroy your Snag Machine, and challenge you to a battle.

The biggest standouts in this battle is her Shadow Mewtwo and Houndoom, both of which have very powerful moves and can easily one shot your Pokemon and are difficult to take down, especially if Aromatisse manages to get the screens up. The good news is that this battle is effectively a 12v6, with Keta as your partner, and the rest of her Pokemon are not too threatening. If you're able to set up on Floette or Golbat, you can easily sweep her team and be ready for Houndoom and Mewtwo. Two of Keta's Pokemon has Fake Out, which you can take advantage of to deal heavy damage. If you manage to take out one of her two aces, turning the battle into a 2v1 will even the odds by a lot.

After the battle, you and Aelita can enter the back door of the previous room. Head over and go down the elevator, making sure to pick up the data for our next battle. After a cutscene you'll find yourself in another Dimensional Rift, and once you step onto the middle platform, a battle will begin.

Dimensional Rift Volcanion Despite being a legendary, this is a 6v1 battle and it doesn't have the strongest of moves. It's also really slow, so you can just chip away at it with your Pokemon advantage. Rock-type, Grass-type, Electric-type, or Ground-type moves will deal heavy damage to it, so you probably have something that will work against it.

Caratos Aftermath

Eldest will ask you to meet at the Celebi statue near the village entrance from Amethyst Cave, so go on ahead and head over there. Touch the statue and be transported to the Spring of Purification. After a short cutscene, you can now purify Pokemon here. Pokemon that are purified from their Shadow forms tend to have extra moves that it can't normally learn through any means. For now, here's the list of Shadow Pokemon that you potentially have. Moves with a * are moves that it normally cannot learn.

Once you're done, our next destination is Goldenwood Town. Step onto the red square to return to Sheridan Village, we can return here by touching the statue at any point in time. Once outside, Keta's spirit will come ask you to meet him in Amethyst Grotto. You can go with him now if you want, and that'll be continued here. There's sidequests we can do now though, including the Help Plaza quests, so if you want to do those first go ahead and say no.
